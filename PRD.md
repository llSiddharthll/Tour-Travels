# Product Requirements Document (PRD) - Himachal Tour & Travels

## Project Overview
Himachal Tour & Travels is a modern, high-converting travel portal dedicated to providing comprehensive travel services in Himachal Pradesh. The application aims to showcase tour packages, taxi services, and regional destinations with a premium user experience and seamless lead generation.

## Objective
*   Create a visually stunning and professional platform for travelers to explore Himachal Pradesh.
*   Provide detailed information on tour packages, destinations, and taxi services.
*   Optimize for lead conversion through clear call-to-actions and contact forms.
*   Ensure full mobile responsiveness and accessibility.

## Target Audience
*   Domestic and international tourists planning a trip to Himachal Pradesh.
*   Business travelers needing reliable taxi services in the region.
*   Adventure seekers looking for curated tour packages.

## Core Features

### 1. Header & Navigation
*   Sticky navigation bar with links to key sections (Home, About, Packages, Taxi, Contact).
*   Dynamic background transitions on scroll.

### 2. Homepage (Hero & Discovery)
*   Impactful hero section with high-quality imagery and backdrop-blur effects.
*   "Difference" section highlighting USP in a professional grid layout.
*   Featured packages or destinations preview.

### 3. Tour Packages
*   Detailed list and individual pages for tour packages.
*   Integration with a database (PostgreSQL) to manage package details (price, duration, itinerary).
*   Visual components for package highlights and inclusions.

### 4. Taxi Services
*   Dedicated section for taxi booking information.
*   Fleet details and regional coverage.

### 5. Destinations
*   Information pages for popular locations (Shimla, Manali, Dharamshala, etc.).
*   Integration with local travel insights.

### 6. About & Contact
*   Professional company profile.
*   Functional contact form for inquiries.
*   Location mapping and direct contact details.

## Technical Stack
*   **Framework**: Next.js 16 (App Router)
*   **Styling**: TailwindCSS 4, Vanilla CSS for custom effects.
*   **Animations**: Framer Motion for smooth transitions and micro-animations.
*   **Slider**: Swiper.js for carousels.
*   **Database**: PostgreSQL for package and service management.
*   **Deployment**: Vercel / GitHub.

## Design Aesthetics
*   **Theme**: Modern, premium, with a focus on vibrant regional imagery.
*   **Typography**: Clean, readable sans-serif fonts (e.g., Inter, Geist).
*   **UI Components**: Glassmorphism effects, smooth gradients, and interactive hover states.

## Success Metrics
*   Page load speed < 2s.
*   High conversion rate on contact forms.
*   Positive user feedback on mobile usability.
